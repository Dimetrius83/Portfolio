My name is Dmitrii. I was born in 1983 in small Hungarian town Keszthely in family of military man and railway engineer. Most of my life I lived in the Moldavian capital Chisinau. In 2005 I've graduated in ATIC (Academy of Transport, Informatics and Comunications) on faculty Computer science on “Programming and applied languages”.

But all of my life I wanted to create. And on one of the lives breaking points I decided to requalify myself to a Designer. Finished courses on 2D and 3D graphics I've started my first job in this domain. I worked in 3 most known design studios in my town.

Another breaking point was pandemic lockdown. I've learned program Blender 3D and finished 170 hours express course “Web development” in University of Ufa of Russian Federation.
